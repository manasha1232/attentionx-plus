The Problem

Content repurposing is broken
for real creators.

Existing tools extract clips and generate captions — but they don't know how you talk. The result? Captions that feel robotic, miss your slang, and tank engagement.

✂️

Manual clip hunting

Creators spend hours scrubbing through footage to find the 60-second golden moment. There's no objective signal — just vibes and experience.

🤖

Generic AI captions

Standard ASR and caption generators produce formal, robotic text. They don't know your slang, your audience's language, or your personal tone.

📉

Code-mixing failures

Creators who naturally mix languages (Hinglish, Spanglish, etc.) watch ASR systems completely mangle their transcript — making captions unusable.

🔄

One-size-fits-all output

No current tool adapts to the individual creator. The same pipeline produces the same output regardless of whether you're a hype creator or a calm educator.
